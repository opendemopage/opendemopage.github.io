A key challenge is to extract disentangled representations of content from the source speech and voice style from the target. In this paper, we present AdaVC, a fast framework which generates speech that accurately resembles the target speaker with minimal loss of content. To achieve this, we propose an adaptive feature extractor that dynamically encodes content and speaker information by effectively utilizing self-supervised speech features. In addition, we leverage a flow enhancer that refines the acoustic representation to further improve speech quality. Subjective and objective evaluations demonstrate that AdaVC synthesizes speech 10 times faster than existing methods with minimal degradation of speech quality.
