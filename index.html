AdaVC: Fast and High Quality Voice Conversion with Adaptive Feature Extractor

Ananymous Submission

Abstract

The goal of this paper is to construct a fast and effective voice conversion framework that converts the speech of a source speaker to sound like a target speaker while preserving linguistic content. A key challenge is to extract disentangled representations of content from the source speech and voice style from the target. In this paper, we present AdaVC, a fast framework which generates speech that accurately resembles the target speaker with minimal loss of content. To achieve this, we propose an adaptive feature extractor that dynamically encodes content and speaker information by effectively utilizing self-supervised speech features. In addition, we leverage a flow enhancer that refines the acoustic representation to further improve speech quality. Subjective and objective evaluations demonstrate that AdaVC synthesizes speech 10 times faster than existing methods with minimal degradation of speech quality.
